About Me

Hello! You might know me as "SmugCroc", but my name is Liz Lorena Berberena. (Hint: my middle name and surname rhyme.) I'm a Cuban-American aspiring front end developer and game developer studying IT and Informatics with a minor in DCIM at Rutgers University in New Brunswick, NJ. I love animals (particularly the small ones), and when not blogging or coding I'm probably drawing for fun, watching anime, dancing, playing computer games, reading about true crime, or coming up with new story ideas. I speak fluent Spanish and English and some French and German. I run a blog and YouTube channel about art, programming, and working on the Web. I have plans for a WordPress theme store and a game I'm working on right now. You can see my projects on Github and Itch.io, see my art or whatever else I'm up to on Twitter!
